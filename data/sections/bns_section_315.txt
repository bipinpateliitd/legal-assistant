BNS Section 315 Illustration: Z dies in possession of furniture and money. His servant A, before the money comes into the possession of any person entitled to such possession, dishonestly misappropriates it. A has committed the offence defined in this section. BNSS Classification Imprisonment for 3 years and fine. If offender was an employee: Imprisonment for 7 years and fine. Non-cognizable Bailable Triable by Magistrate of the first class.
