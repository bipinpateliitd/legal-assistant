BNS Section 95 Explanation: Hiring, employing, engaging or using a child for sexual exploitation or pornography is covered within the meaning of this section. BNSS Classification Imprisonment for not less than 3 years, but may extend to 10 years, and with fine; If offence be committed, additional punishment as provided for that offence. Cognizable Non-Bailable Triable by Magistrate of the first class.
