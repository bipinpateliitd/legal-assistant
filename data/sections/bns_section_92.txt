BNS Section 92 Illustration: A, knowing that he is likely to cause the death of a pregnant woman, does an act which, if it caused the death of the woman, would amount to culpable homicide. The woman is injured, but does not die; but the death of an unborn quick child with which she is pregnant is thereby caused. A is guilty of the offence defined in this section. BNSS Classification Imprisonment for 10 years and fine. Cognizable Non-bailable Triable by Court of Session
