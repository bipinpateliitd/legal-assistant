BNS Section 207 - shall be punished with simple imprisonment for a term which may extend to one month or with fine which may extend to five thousand rupees, or with both; where the summons, notice, order or proclamation is to attend in person or by agent, or to produce a document or electronic record in a Court with simple imprisonment for a term which may extend to six months, or with fine which may extend to ten thousand rupees, or with both.
