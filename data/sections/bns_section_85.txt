BNS Section 85 BNSS Classification Imprisonment for 3 years and fine. Cognizable if information relating to the commission of the offence is given to an officer in charge of a police station by the person aggrieved by the offence or by any person related to her by blood, marriage or adoption or if there is no such relative, by any public servant belonging to such class or category as may be notified by the State Government in this behalf. Non-bailable Triable by Magistrate of the first class
