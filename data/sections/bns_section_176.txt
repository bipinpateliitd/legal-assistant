BNS Section 176 Provided that if any person having incurred any such expenses not exceeding the amount of ten rupees without authority obtains within ten days from the date on which such expenses were incurred the approval in writing of the candidate, he shall be deemed to have incurred such expenses with the authority of the candidate. BNSS Classification Fine of 10,000 rupees. Non-cognizable Bailable Triable by Magistrate of the first class.
