BNS Section 344 Explanation: It shall be sufficient in any charge under this section to allege a general intent to defraud without naming any particular person intended to be defrauded or specifying any particular sum of money intended to be the subject of the fraud, or any particular day on which the offence was committed. BNSS Classification Imprisonment for 7 years, or fine, or both. Non-cognizable Bailable Triable by Magistrate of the first class.
