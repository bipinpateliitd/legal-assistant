BNS Section 240 Explanation: In sections 236 and 237 and in this section the word “offence” includes any act committed at any place out of India, which, if committed in India, would be punishable under any of the following sections, namely, 97, 99, 172, 173, 174, 175, 301, 304, 305, 306, 320, 325 and 326. BNSS Classification Imprisonment for 2 years, or fine, or both. Non-cognizable Bailable Triable by Any Magistrate.
